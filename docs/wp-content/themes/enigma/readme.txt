== Description ==
Enigma is an outstanding superfine creation from Vibhor Purandare, Enigma has become one of the most popular multipurpose responsive theme with WPML lovers by many professionals for its amazing capabilities and great designs. Enigma has Flickr as a custom widget to showing up your Flickr Photos .Enigma provide you a platform for business template, awesome BOOTSTRAP Carousel slider for full CMS control option to match your logo & website, wide layout and many more.
