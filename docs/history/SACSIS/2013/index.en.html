SACSIS2013

SACSIS2013
May 22 - 24, 2013 at Sendai International Center, Sendai, Japan　

　SACSIS (Symposium on Advanced Computing Systems and Infrastructures) , the Annual Symposium on Advanced Computing Systems and Infrastructures, is a symposium for discussing advanced computing systems, infrastructures, and technologies for enabling them from wide and various viewpoints. It covers both technologies on "systems" and technologies on "infrastructures." The 11th symposium will be held in Sendai city from May 22th through May 24th, 2013.
