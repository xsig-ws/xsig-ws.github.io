ACSI2016 Call for Posters

We invite the submission of proposals for poster presentations at ACSI2016. Our scope includes all areas of advanced computing systems and infrastructure. Posters may describe concepts of your research, results of work-in-progress, problems to be solved, etc.

[Submission]

  • The poster submissions must be A4-size PDF format.
  • Acceptable language is only "English".
  • It is allowed to submit a Japanese or English 1 - 2 pages support document (NOT MANDATORY) .
    This is used in poster review as a reference.
  • If you require a special environment such as a desk and power supply, please contact to the organizing committee.
  • Visit "Submission page" and follow the instruction.

[Accepted Posters]

  • Panel size is 122cm(W) x 180cm(H).
  • Thumbtacks will be prepared.
  • Required to give an one-min. presentation for indexing.
  • Full registration is required for at least one presenter.
  • Posters (A4-size PDF version) are distributed only to attendees and not opened to the public.

[Award]

  • ACSI2016 program committee selects at most one "ACSI2016 Outstanding Poster Award"
